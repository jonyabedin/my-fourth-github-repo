Natural Beauty of Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a very beautiful country with many rivers, green fields, big forests, and lovely hills. The rivers like Padma and Jamuna make the land rich for farming. The Sundarbans is a famous forest where the Royal Bengal Tiger lives. In Sylhet, there are green tea gardens, and Cox’s Bazar is the longest sea beach in the world. Many birds, animals, and flowers can be seen everywhere. The villages look nice with many trees and ponds. All these things make Bangladesh special. We should take care of our country’s nature so it stays beautiful forever.
